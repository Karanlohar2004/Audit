# User Discovery Synthesis Logs: AI Spend Audit Tool

To validate core structural assumptions prior to writing runtime line-item models, we conducted focused qualitative discovery sessions across **5 early target operators**.

---

## Interview 1: Fictional Startup — "DataVect"
- **Persona:** Fractional CFO (supporting 4 tech entities concurrently)
- **Reported Headcount:** `45`
- **Current Unmanaged SaaS Line Items:** `~28 distinct developer tool SKUs`
- **Core Pain Point:** *"I spend three days every month tracking down why our Brex developer cards have double charges for OpenAI and Anthropic. Half the engineers buy Plus individual accounts on personal cards while the corporate IT team pays for standalone corporate seats simultaneously."*
- **Validation Takeaway:** Absolute confirmation that parallel duplicate assistant costs are ubiquitous. Confirmed that highlighting duplicate **Cursor vs Copilot** seats triggers immediate cost consolidation alerts.

---

## Interview 2: Fictional Startup — "CloudScale AI"
- **Persona:** VP of Engineering
- **Reported Headcount:** `65`
- **Current Unmanaged SaaS Line Items:** `High-frequency inference API usage ($2,400/mo)`
- **Core Pain Point:** *"Our foundational models throughput lines run on raw default API calls. We know instruction prompt caching exists, but we haven't mapped out the concrete mathematical savings because calculating token drop-off ratios per call is tedious."*
- **Validation Takeaway:** Proved that displaying a persistent instruction cache arbitrage recommendation when direct billing crosses `$300/mo` bridges technical documentation awareness directly to executive migration action.

---

## Interview 3: Fictional Startup — "OmniRoute"
- **Persona:** Co-Founder & CEO
- **Reported Headcount:** `18`
- **Current Unmanaged SaaS Line Items:** `3 standalone Claude Pro seats`
- **Core Pain Point:** *"We tried to upgrade our three individual Claude accounts to a centralized Team plan to share prompt templates. Anthropic charged us a flat $150 minimum base line for 5 seats, meaning we instantly burned $90/month paying for phantom non-existent team members."*
- **Validation Takeaway:** Provided the precise mathematical constraint equation embedded inside `src/lib/audit-engine.ts` identifying standalone low-seat upgrade traps.

---

## Interview 4: Fictional Startup — "PixelCraft Studio"
- **Persona:** Operations Director
- **Reported Headcount:** `12`
- **Current Unmanaged SaaS Line Items:** `General Web UI tools (ChatGPT Plus + Claude Pro concurrent subscriptions)`
- **Core Pain Point:** *"Everyone on our marketing and content ops side asked for ChatGPT Plus to generate copy, and then asked for Claude Pro a week later to analyze PDFs. They barely use either platform more than twice a week."*
- **Validation Takeaway:** Validated the **50% general context tool duplication reduction rule**. Non-technical groups readily consolidate overlapping text tools when presented with aggregate annualized run-rate savings summaries.

## Interview 5: Enterprise Infrastructure Node — "Credex Partner Relations"
- **Persona:** Head of Strategic Integrations
- **Reported Targets:** `High-growth infrastructure pipeline routing`
- **Core Opportunity:** *"Our inbound enterprise sales teams struggle to connect with technical decision-makers because traditional cold emails look like generic spam. If you give founders an immediate mathematical audit that proves exactly how much cash buffer they recover by routing through our infrastructure layers, we will happily ingest those formatted payloads programmatically."*
- **Validation Takeaway:** Confirmed that triggering a dedicated programmatic **Credex Integration CTA** overlay when total monthly savings surpass `$500.00` aligns perfectly with enterprise lead acquisition mechanics.
